Our Programs

Casa (Ages 3–5)

Our Casa program nurtures early childhood development through hands‑on, sensory‑based learning. Children build independence, concentration, and foundational skills in a warm, structured environment guided by Montessori principles.

Primary Grade School

Designed for ages 6–12, this program emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and self‑directed learning. Students explore language, math, science, and culture through interactive materials and collaborative projects.

Junior High School

Our Junior High curriculum integrates academic rigor with character formation. Learners deepen their understanding of core subjects while developing leadership, communication, and problem‑solving skills.

Senior High School

Senior High students choose specialized tracks aligned with their interests and career goals. The program blends advanced academics with vocational training, preparing students for college, entrepreneurship, or professional life.

About O.B. Montessori

O.B. Montessori Center is a premier educational institution in the Philippines, founded on the principles of Dr. Maria Montessori. We nurture students to become confident, competent, and compassionate leaders through a holistic curriculum that blends academic excellence with character development. Our digital platform empowers learners and educators with modern tools for seamless collaboration and growth.

Visionaries and Founders

Maria Montessori

Founder of the Montessori Method, she is an Italian physician and educator who revolutionized early childhood education with her child-centered approach.

Mario Montessori Sr.

Maria Montessori’s son and closest collaborator, who helped expand Montessori education globally and continue his mother’s legacy.

Preciosa Soliven

Founder of Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center, she brought the Montessori philosophy to the Philippines and promoted holistic education for Filipino youth.

Oscar Arellano

Foundational figure in the history of OBMC schools as he headed the board it was under.

Admissions

At O.B. Montessori Center, we welcome students who are eager to grow in a nurturing and academically rigorous environment. Our admissions process is designed to identify learners who will thrive under the Montessori method and contribute positively to our community. We offer rolling admissions for Casa to Senior High School levels, with entrance assessments and orientation programs to ensure a smooth transition.

Step 1: Inquiry & Campus Tour

Schedule a visit or attend our Open House to experience the Montessori learning environment firsthand.

Step 2: Application & Assessment

Submit your child’s documents online or at the campus. A short assessment and interview will follow.

Step 3: Enrollment & Orientation

Once accepted, families receive orientation materials to prepare for the Montessori journey ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the O.B. Montessori Digital Platform?
It’s an online portal that allows students and parents to access announcements, schedules, grades, and school resources easily from any device.
How do I log in?
Log in using your school‑issued email and password. If you’ve forgotten your credentials, click “Forgot Password?” to reset them.
Can parents and students use the same account?
No. Only students have accounts to view their grades and answer assesments.
Is my information secure?
Absolutely. The platform follows strict data protection standards and uses encrypted connections to keep all user data safe.
